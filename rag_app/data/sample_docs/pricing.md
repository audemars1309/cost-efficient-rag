# Nimbus Cloud Storage — Pricing

## Plans

Nimbus offers three plans: Free, Pro, and Business.

The Free plan includes 5 GB of storage, file versioning for the last 7 days, and access from one device at a time. It is intended for individual light use and cannot be used for team folders.

The Pro plan costs $9.99 per month or $99 per year (a 17% discount versus monthly billing). Pro includes 2 TB of storage, 30 days of file versioning, access from unlimited devices, and priority email support with a 24-hour response target.

The Business plan costs $18 per user per month, billed annually, with a minimum of 3 seats. Business includes unlimited storage, 180 days of file versioning, admin console with team-wide access controls, SSO via SAML, and a dedicated support contact with a 4-hour response target during business hours.

## Refund policy

Nimbus offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on Pro annual subscriptions only. Monthly Pro subscriptions and all Business subscriptions are non-refundable but can be cancelled at any time, with access continuing until the end of the current billing period.

## Storage overage

If a Pro user exceeds their 2 TB quota, uploads are paused (not deleted) until the user either frees up space or upgrades. Nimbus does not charge overage fees on the Pro plan. Business plans have no storage cap, so overage does not apply.
